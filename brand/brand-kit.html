The Panes system.

Split Focus gives Panes a recognizable window. Terminal mint marks the active surface. A lowercase Proxima Nova wordmark keeps the identity compact and approachable.

This kit separates brand assets from product UI tokens so both can evolve without losing the relationship between them.

One icon from dock to title bar.

The app icon uses a solid dark squircle, a neutral Split Focus mark, and one mint active pane. There is no texture, gradient, or glow to fight the symbol at small sizes.

Brand type and product type have different jobs.

Proxima Nova appears only in the outlined wordmark. Geist remains the application typeface, and Geist Mono remains reserved for code, commands, hashes, and compact metadata.

Proxima Nova Alt Bold, lowercase, converted to portable vector outlines. Do not typeset the wordmark manually inside the app.

One cockpit for work around the code.

Geist carries navigation, conversation, changes, approvals, and settings. Use weight and scale before adding another typeface.
